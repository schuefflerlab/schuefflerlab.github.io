## Teaching

We participate in the graduate program offered by the TUM, including:

- the [TUM Graduate School](https://www.gs.tum.de/en/gs/tum-graduate-school/)
- the [TUM Medical Graduate Center](https://www.mgc.med.tum.de/en)

We are also part of the [AI in Biomedicine – Elite Master's Program](https://www.cit.tum.de/en/cit/studies/degree-programs/ai-in-biomedicine/).

For all semester dates, please refer to the [TUM Academic Calendar](https://www.tum.de/en/studies/application/application-info-portal/dates-periods-and-deadlines).
